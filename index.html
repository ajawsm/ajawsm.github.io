Season's Greetings from BROOKVILLE!

In 2020, BROOKVILLE - like all businesses - found new challenges amid the COVID-19 pandemic. While we carefully restored operations with new health and safety protocols after a brief suspension beginning in March, we were fortunate to continue supporting our customers across each of our primary industries this year with a variety of unique projects. Thank you for giving us the opportunity to serve you in this unprecedented year. We hope that you and yours have a holiday season filled with good health and great joy, and we look forward to collaborating again in 2021.

For more on BROOKVILLE's unique year in 2020, please hover over or tap the photos below!

BROOKVILLE developed and delivered two new Incline Railway Vehicles for CARTA's Lookout Mountain Incline Railway in Chattanooga, Tenn.
BROOKVILLE is currently working through a 35-unit order for a comprehensive rail support fleet of locomotives, personnel carriers, and maintenance vehicles at Arch Resources' new Leer South Mine in West Virginia.
BROOKVILLE implemented extra health and safety measures, including face masks, temperature screenings, and social distancing to combat the spread of COVID-19.
BROOKVILLE wrapped up delivery of eight MBTA propulsion and truck system upgrades for MBTA's PCC streetcar fleet in Boston.
BROOKVILLE shipped the last vehicle (pictured) of a 4-year, 16-unit PCC streetcar rebuild for SFMTA in San Francisco.
Liberty NXT Streetcar programs for Valley Metro's Tempe Streetcar, Sound Transit's Tacoma Link, and Portland Streetcar continued to progress. Shipments to Tempe will commence in 2021.

Season's Greetings from BROOKVILLE!

In 2019, BROOKVILLE progressed major transit and locomotive projects, continued deliveries for rebuild and new equipment programs in the mining industry, and served customers in new ways with maintenance services for Liberty Streetcar fleets. Thank you for giving us the opportunity to serve you this year. We hope that you and yours have a holiday season filled with joy and happiness, and we look forward to working together in 2020.

For more on BROOKVILLE's big year in 2019, please hover over or tap the photos below!

BROOKVILLE shipped three PCC Streetcars to SFMTA in San Francisco as part of a 16 car overhaul program.
This streetcar, SFMTA Car No. 1057, and Car No. 1058 (above) are single-enders, with operator controls at one end - great for operation on a rail loop.
SFMTA Car No. 1015 is a double-ender, with operator controls at both ends, ideal for rail lines. All three of the vehicles shipped this year were originally manufactured in the 1940s.
BROOKVILLE joined PA Trolley Museum in Washington, Pa. to celebrate the completion of the restoration of West Penn Railways Car No. 832. BROOKVILLE's contributions include carbody and truck restoration, refinishing, and replacing wiring and piping.
BROOKVILLE continued to deliver new and rebuilt mining locomotives, personnel carriers, and specialty equipment for its customers in 2019.
In April, BROOKVILLE honored team members celebrating major 10-year and 20-year service milestones with a luncheon and awards ceremony.
In November, BROOKVILLE's Alisha Adaway, Transdev's Terry Mulachy, and Milwaukee Alderman Robert Bauman spoke to the NRHS Wisconsin Chapter about The Hop Streetcar.
